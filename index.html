ProDiGi Project Idea

We are working under the domain: Health and safety of workers

About Project

When we were discussing the problem statement we got various sub points where we can work for the health and safety of the workers, but we have noticed there are three most important things where we can work on. We came across gist of the ideas. We have discussed the ideas and we have decided to work on the following three points.

  • Crack Detection in Machines and Structures
  • Safety Parameter Detection
  • Safety Sensors

Let's Talk About These Features in Detail

Crack Detection in Machines and Structures

Crack detection is a very important aspect of the utility based industry. Cracks in the building can lead to major structural damage. Similarly, cracks in the roads, equipment, etc can lead to major damage. To resolve this issue, the following can be done:

  • A crack detection system can be implemented to detect cracks in the building, roads, equipment, etc
  • Cracks in container vehicle can be detected and the driver can be alerted to avoid any hazardous situation
  • Cracks in the roads can be detected and thus the road can be repaired before any major damage occurs.

Safety Parameter Detection

Working as a onsite field worker. Its equally important to ensure that the worker is safe and is not in any danger. The various issues that can worker can face are:

  • High voltage areas
  • High noise levels
  • High temperatures
  • High humidity
  • High dust levels
  • High radiation levels
  • High vibration levels
To resolve this issue, the following can be done: A realtime monitoring system can be implemented to monitor the various safety parameters of the worker (eg- Safety Googles,Helmet, gloves, shoes, etc). The data can be analysed and the worker can be alerted if the parameters are not within the safe limits

Safety Sensors

New maintenance techniques are now possible because to the availability of new sensors that are intended to gauge the health and performance of equipment.

A motor or conveyor, for instance, may contain numerous vibration sensors. Motors that have recently undergone maintenance, new bearings, and proper alignment should vibrate very little. As the components wear, the vibration increases until it reaches a threshold level. At this point, an automatic alert can trigger further investigation.
